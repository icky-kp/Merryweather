About Merryweather Security

Merryweather Security comprises several divisions:

  • Security Division

    Provides on-site and property protection with uniformed guards, often patrolling premises with guard dogs or security vehicles.

  • Paramilitary Force

    Highly trained operatives for high-risk operations, with three distinct uniform variants:

    • Blackops 1: Light gear, caps with logos, shades, polo shirts, camo pants, and boots.
    • Blackops 2: Heavier equipment, military caps or ski hats, sunglasses, jackets or polo shirts, bulletproof vests, tactical pants, and boots.
    • Blackops 3: Black operations unit, identical to Blackops 2 but without hats, night vision gear.

  • Chemsec Division

    Similar to the security division but with vests having a red outline and "SECURITY" written on the back, operating around Humane Labs and Research.

  • Courier Service

    Transports art, artifacts, and perishables for museums and collectors.

Merryweather primarily recruits ex-military personnel, ensuring a well-skilled mercenary force.
